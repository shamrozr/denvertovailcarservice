The Luxury Spots

The Broadmoor
Nestled in the foothills of the Rocky Mountains, The Broadmoor is a legendary resort that offers its guests an extraordinary way to experience the unique beauty of the American West. With Eddie Limo, you can arrive at this stunning destination in style and comfort.

Cherry Creek Shopping District
For those who love to shop, the Cherry Creek Shopping District is a must-visit. Home to an impressive collection of high-end brands, it’s a shopper’s paradise. With Eddie Limo’s shopping package, you can shop till you drop without worrying about transportation.

Denver Art Museum
Art enthusiasts will love the Denver Art Museum, which boasts an incredible array of exhibits. Our museum tour service ensures you arrive in style and on time for your cultural exploration.

Denver’s dining scene is vibrant and diverse, with something to satisfy every palate. From the sushi masterpieces of Matsuhisa to the mouthwatering steaks at Guard and Grace, Eddie Limo’s dinner package ensures a perfect end to your day.
