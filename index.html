WHO WE ARE?

Eddie Limo started like most startup businesses in our modern-day society; with a limited budget and a shortlist of customers. Because of the limited budget of the company, Eddie Limo started with one vehicle in its fleet. But as the business began to cater to clients' needs through premium group transportation services, private car services, and luxury black car services, it quickly became the most sought-after limousine service in Colorado. Now, with a fleet of more than 10 luxury cars, Eddie Limo is the premier destination for luxurious Limo services and airport transportation in Denver, Colorado
