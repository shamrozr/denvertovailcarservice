About Us

Eddie Limo's journey began as many startups do in today's world: on a tight budget with just a few clients. Initially operating with a single vehicle, the company swiftly rose to prominence in Colorado by offering outstanding group, private, and luxury black car transportation services. Today, Eddie Limo boasts a fleet of over 10 high-end vehicles, establishing itself as the go-to provider for top-tier limousine and airport transportation services in Denver, Colorado.

OUR PRIVATE CAR SERVICE HISTORY

Established in 2003 by Grigor Aghabalyan, Eddie Limo has become a leading name in Colorado's limousine service sector. Prior to founding the company, Grigor, often referred to as George, worked for a major limousine service in Denver. His experiences there sparked the inspiration to start his own venture, aiming to elevate the standard of professional transportation for commuters, business travelers, and tourists alike.

OWNER'S BACKGROUND

George's journey to the American dream began in Yerevan, Armenia, and led him to the United States after university. His pursuit of a better life involved various jobs across different sectors until he found his calling in the limousine industry. It was here that George discovered his passion not just for living the American dream, but for facilitating it for others through exceptional chauffeuring services, thus leading to the birth of Eddie Limo.

BUSINESS BACKGROUND

Mirroring the start of many modern startups, Eddie Limo began with modest means and a limited customer base. The company quickly grew out of its humble beginnings, expanding its services to include luxurious group and private transportation. This growth trajectory propelled Eddie Limo to the forefront of Colorado’s limousine services, now boasting an impressive fleet of luxury vehicles.

OUR MISSION

Eddie Limo is dedicated to providing unparalleled limousine services in Denver, a commitment that has led to numerous nominations and awards, including being a top-rated company in 2018 and 2019. The company's success is rooted in its consistent delivery of excellent airport transportation and limousine services, earning it both local and national recognition for its excellence.

OUR SUCCESS STORY

Eddie Limo’s remarkable growth from a single car to a fleet of over 10 luxury vehicles, including SUVs, stretch limos, and sprinter vans, reflects our dedication to customer satisfaction. This success is a testament to our commitment to providing premium transportation solutions, and although there is still more to achieve, we are proud of how far we have come.

Our Promise

At Eddie Limo, we pride ourselves on delivering top-notch transportation services, with a focus on professionalism, reliability, and discretion. Our commitment to your comfort and safety is unwavering, and we are dedicated to customizing our services to meet your specific needs.

Eddie Limo During COVID-19

During the challenges of the COVID-19 pandemic, Eddie Limo has adapted to continue providing safe and reliable limousine services. By adhering to strict sanitation and social distancing protocols, including regular vehicle sanitization and health checks for drivers, we ensure the highest safety standards for our clients. We are committed to your safety and are ready to serve your transportation needs during these trying times.
